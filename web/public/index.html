Each team gets a strength index from public data — national Elo, club market value, and translated club performance (predicted VAEP). A regularised logistic model turns index gaps into win/draw/loss, a Dixon-Coles model into scorelines, and 20,000 Monte Carlo runs of the bracket into the stage odds, refit after every result.

Pre-registered honestly: the model is calibrated (ECE 0.054) and lands within ~0.017 Brier of the closing market, ahead of Elo — but the club-talent thesis it tests is not supported. Odds are distributions over model uncertainty, never point bets, and betting odds are never an input. Code & method on GitHub.
